About Us

In recent years, advancements in medical technology and the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) have ushered in a new era in healthcare. One significant area of focus has been detection cum diagnosis of brain tumours, a critical task that demands accuracy and timeliness for effective treatment. With the convergence of algorithms and telemedicine, a groundbreaking approach has emerged, promising to transform the landscape of brain tumour detection.

The aim of our project is to explore the synergistic potential of algorithm-based brain tumour detection and telemedicine, offering a comprehensive solution for improved diagnosis and timely intervention. By implementing machine learning algorithms capable of analyzing medical imaging data, we can automate the initial screening process, reducing the time required for accurate diagnosis. The incorporation of telemedicine platforms allows for remote consultations, enabling patients in underserved areas to access expert opinions and recommendations without needing to travel.
